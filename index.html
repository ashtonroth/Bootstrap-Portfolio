A Little About Ashton

Ashton is a motivated individual who seeks to foster deep connections with every single human he interacts with. Ashton has been a teacher, a farmer, a nanny and a market researcher. All of this experience has lent itself to Ashton being able to best adapt to all situations-- even the world of web development. Ashton's goal is to utilize the skills learned from all of his lived experience in order to create useful and practical applications that help to foster deeper connections throughout the world.
